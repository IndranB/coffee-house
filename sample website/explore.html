HOT COFFEE

Hot coffee is a popular beverage enjoyed by many people around the world. It can be made using various methods, including drip brewing, espresso machines, French press, or pour-over techniques.
Some common types of hot coffee include black coffee, espresso, cappuccino, latte, and Americano. People often customize flavored syrups, or other ingredients to suit their taste.

ESPRESSO COFFEE

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee under high pressure. It's a small but potent shot of coffee,typically around 1 ounce (30 milliliters). The process results in a strong and rich flavor with a layer of crema, a creamy, golden-brown foam on top.
Espresso serves as the base for various coffee drinks like lattes.
